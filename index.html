Current TEER planning depends on 2D transesophageal echocardiography and the interventionalist's mental model of complex 3D anatomy. Without patient-specific simulation, clip placement is often iterative — leading to prolonged procedure times, suboptimal hemodynamic outcomes, and increased risk of residual regurgitation. TEER CDSS bridges this gap with real-time 3D simulation directly from patient imaging data.

Core Capabilities

Full-Stack Simulation Platform

From patient imaging to hemodynamic prediction — every step is integrated, interactive, and real-time.

Patient-Specific 3D Reconstruction

Segment real 3D TEE imaging into patient-specific mitral valve meshes using the MVSeg2023 deep learning pipeline. 105 validated patient geometries included.

Real-Time Fluid-Structure Interaction

Physics-based blood flow simulation with Marching Cubes isosurface rendering, BVH continuous raycasting collision, and Newtonian particle dynamics.

MitraClip G4 Virtual Deployment

Place, reposition, and evaluate clip effects on double-orifice flow and residual regurgitation in real-time. Full 6-DOF articulated NT G4 CAD model.

Hemodynamic Telemetry Dashboard

Live LA/LV pressure waveforms, transmitral flow rate, peak regurgitation volume tracking, and real-time cardiac phase indicators — all updating at 60fps.
